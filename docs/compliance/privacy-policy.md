## 1. Who this policy covers

- **Prospective families** who submit an inquiry.
- **Parents and guardians** who create an account and enroll a student.
- **Students**, including minors, whose information is provided by their parent or guardian.

## 2. Information we collect

**You provide to us:**

- **Contact and account information:** name, email address, phone number, time zone, and profile photo (optional).
- **Inquiry information:** the prospective student's name/age, experience level, preferred lesson length, preferred days and times, and any message you send us.
- **Student information:** student name, experience level, preferred lesson duration, and lesson notes. This information is provided by the parent/guardian account holder.
- **Scheduling information:** lessons booked, times, and related notes.
- **Payment information:** invoice amounts and payment status, and, when you pay online, payment details you enter directly with our payment processor (see Section 4). We do **not** store your full card number on our systems.
- **Bank-account connection (planned):** if you choose to connect a bank account to pay tuition, you will do so through **Plaid**. Plaid collects your banking credentials and account information directly and shares limited account and payment information with us as needed to process payments. We do not receive or store your online-banking password. See Plaid's end-user privacy policy at https://plaid.com/legal/#end-user-privacy-policy.

**Collected automatically:**

- Basic technical data needed to operate and secure the Service (e.g., authentication session data, and standard server/log information from our hosting provider). We do not use advertising trackers.

**From third parties:**

- If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email) from Google to create or access your account.

## 3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

- Respond to inquiries and evaluate enrollment.
- Create and manage accounts and student profiles.
- Schedule and manage lessons.
- Issue invoices and process tuition payments.
- Send service communications (e.g., scheduling notices, account and payment notifications, including browser push notifications if you enable them).
- Secure the Service, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

## 4. Payments

Online card payments are processed by **Stripe**. Bank-account connections (once available) are handled by **Plaid**. When you make a payment, you provide your payment details directly to these processors, who handle that data under their own privacy policies:

- Stripe — https://stripe.com/privacy
- Plaid — https://plaid.com/legal/#end-user-privacy-policy

We receive confirmation of payment and limited, non-sensitive transaction details necessary to reconcile your account.

## 5. Children's privacy

Piano students may be minors. **Accounts are created and controlled by a parent or guardian**, who provides any information about their child. We do not knowingly allow children to create their own accounts, and we collect a child's information only from the supervising parent/guardian for the purpose of providing lessons.

We do not use children's information for advertising or sell it. A parent or guardian may review, update, or request deletion of their child's information at any time by contacting us at [FILL-IN: privacy@abamusicacademy.org]. If you believe a child provided us information without parental involvement, contact us and we will delete it.

We share personal information only as follows:

- **Service providers (sub-processors)** who process data on our behalf, listed below.

## 7. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, or as required by law. Details are in our Data Retention and Deletion Policy; in summary, we retain account and student records while the account is active and for a limited period afterward, and payment/invoice records for the period required for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion as described in Section 9.

## 8. Security

We protect personal information using encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest, role-based access with Row Level Security, and multi-factor authentication for administrative access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information and to respond promptly to any incident. See our Information Security Policy for details.
